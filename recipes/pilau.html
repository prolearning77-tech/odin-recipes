Pilau Recipe

Courtesy: Yummy Mardley

Description

Ingredients

  • 3 cups rice
  • 1 kg beef, cut into small pieces
  • 1 tablespoon pilau mix
  • 2 large onions, sliced
  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 potatoes (optional), peeled and cubed
  • 1-inch piece ginger, grafted
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 4 cups beef stock or water
  • 3 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt to taste

Pilau spices

  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp cardamon
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp cloves
  • 1 small cinnamon stick

Method

  1. Cut the beef into medium size pieces
  2. Wash the meat properly
  3. Put the meat in a cooking pot and add little water
  4. Boil the meat with medium heat
  5. While boiling the meat prepare other ingredients
